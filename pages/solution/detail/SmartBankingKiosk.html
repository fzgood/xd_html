Solutions>Smart Service>Smart Banking Kiosk

In many self-service scenarios, financial services scenarios account for a large share. With the development of technology, more and more businesses can be completed by the customer on the terminal, eliminating the irritability of queuing in front of the counter, saving work time and improving work efficiency. Emperor Technology's smart bank self-service cabinet can already complete the business of most banks, including new user account opening, card extension, bank card application and instant card making.

The smart bank self-service cabinet, as a supplement to traditional ATM, is loved by major banks. The efficiency of the bank's work has also won more customers and business for them. For different bank sizes, Emperor can provide different types of self-service cabinets. In addition, we can provide customers with customized software systems to meet different business needs.
