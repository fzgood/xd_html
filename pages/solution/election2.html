Election

Elections are a very important activity in a democratic country, and people express their support and opinions to the government in this free and fair manner. At present, the way of elections is gradually changing, from traditional manual voting and counting to electronic voting and counting. Voter cards have also involved more biometric personalization information, eliminating the possibility of cheating in voting.

Emperor Tech has very rich experience in electoral projects. We provided voter cards and personalization equipment for two Nigerian general elections, and won the PMI Project Management Excellence Award. The company also provides electronic voting machines for Kaduna's state election, marking the successful application of electronic elections in Africa and providing voters with a better electoral experience.

Giving everyone a fair and just election system is the direction we have been working hard.
