Solutions>Smart Service>Automatic Fare Collection

More convenience, easier operations, more efficient collections. Those impressions are already deep in people’s heart since the implementation of automatic fare collection (AFC). Indeed, AFC has brought a lot of benefits to both public transport operators and citizens.

AFC system can greatly increase the convenience in the city and improve its image. We are very proud that Emperor Tech’s AFC solution has been deployed for over 140 cities in China with great success. And we can hear positive voice from passengers that they enjoy the seamless travel experience very much, the solution makes their lives easier.

Our AFC supports multiple payment methods like bus pass, NFC (Apple Pay), QR code, and banking card, thus to meet the needs of all passengers，We developed many equipment for AFC solution and we will introduce them in detail in validator solution.

AFC system can cover many scenarios such as bus, rail, ferry, road toll, parking, fast-food restaurants and convenience stores. Our solution is fully applicable to all forms of transportation in different scales.
