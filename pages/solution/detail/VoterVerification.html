Voter Verification

Voter verification is the most important step before the election, which ensures the transparency and creditability during the whole election process. There are many problems in the traditional manual voter verification, such as time-consuming and tampering data. The voters doubt about creditability of the voting process.

The additional biometric information encrypted in the voter card improves the security and creditability of elections, but at the same time, it also adds a lot of work to the process of voter verification. The traditional election process needs to be gradually transformed to electronic process. And the election commission needs an efficient verification device to solve this problem.

Refer to election scenarios in different countries, we developed two verification devices, which are satisfied with the voter verification process of fixed scenarios and mobile scenarios. The voter information that needs to be verified will be compared with the background database in real time through wireless network, and any data that does not exist in the database cannot pass the verification process. With the help of voter verifier, the whole validation process takes only a few minutes.
