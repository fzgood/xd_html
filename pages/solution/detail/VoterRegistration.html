Voter Registration

Elections have been the usual mechanism by which democracies have operated since the 17th century. It is a formal decision-making process in which people vote for a candidate for a particular position or for an entire organization. Elections are often defined as a formal expression of the wishes of the governed, which are combined into a collective decision to decide who can govern or who can stay in power.

Elections are a democratic process, qualified voters have the right to vote, and government power in a democracy comes from the consent of the people to be governed. So every election is a free and fair way for people to express their consent to the power of government.

Traditional voter registration is usually done by the election commission with information collection of qualified voters. With the popularity of electronic voting systems, artificial voter registration has been past. Emperor Tech can provide a range of enrollment equipment including fixed point registration equipment and mobile enrollment equipment designed for people in remote areas or with limited mobility. All information input will be verified and uploaded to the voter database, which truly achieving the principle of one person, one vote.
