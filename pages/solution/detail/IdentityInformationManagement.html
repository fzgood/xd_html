Solutions>Smart Service>Identity Information Management

Emperor Tech Identity Information Management System is a software for managing personally identifiable information. It is usually used in conjunction with our ID document personalization system to jointly produce different types of ID documents. Everyone's information is stored in the system's database, and AES 256-bit encryption algorithm is used to ensure data security. The database will be automatically backed up regularly to prevent system anomalies.

The system can comprehensively manage customer information, such as information registration, modification, and verification. The information collected by the front end will be directly transmitted to the platform through the Internet after encryption, and then the database will be automatically synchronized. The introduction of the identity information management system has reduced the use of labor costs and paper documents, avoiding errors caused by manual input. Now the system has been applied in many countries.

Technically the system is based on the concept of an enterprise service bus (ESB). Within a lightweight and high-performance Enterprise Service Bus (ESB), the system provides exceptional support for XML, Web Services and REST. The wide range of transport adapters available for the Identity Information Management System, enables it to communicate over many application and transport layer protocols, like HTTP(S), Mail (POP3, IMAP, SMTP), JMS, TCP, UDP, VFS, SMS, XMPP and FIX.

The system uses a flexible architecture based on SOA principles such as loose-coupling and asynchronous message passing, emphasizing an incremental approach to adopting and deploying an SOI.

Identity Information Management

The whole solution contains 8 subsystems, which are implemented: Application & Issuance Offices, Central Information System, and Personalization Center.

There are 2 subsystems implemented include Application & Issuance Offices: Data Collection System and Document Issuance System.

Under the Central Information System process for subsystem: Data Preparation System, Monitoring System, Report System, Administration and Audit System.
