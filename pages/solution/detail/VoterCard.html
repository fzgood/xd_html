Solutions>Election>Voter Card

Voter Cards enable registered voters to exercise their civic right to vote in general elections. Any legal resident has the right to vote. After voter registration, everyone will receive his own voter card issued by the Election Commission. When election is being held, qualified voters go to the voting station with their own voter cards, after successful verification, they can vote. Voter Cards in some countries can also serve as general identity for other purposes, such as buying a SIM card or used as travel document, as they contain all basic information like name, age and address inside.

With the continuous development of technology, the security of original voter card no longer meets the needs, and the voter card which containing only basic information may be counterfeited, that violates the election principle of “One person, One vote”. Emperor Tech’s voter card solution adds more biometric information of voters, such as fingerprints, iris and face information, greatly improving the security of voter cards. Voters are allowed to vote only when he is verified to be the same as the card holder, which truly realizes “One person, One vote.
