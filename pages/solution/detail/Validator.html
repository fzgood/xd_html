Solutions>Smart Service>Validator

As a part of the AFC system, the validator plays an important role. Our validators are developed with the cutting-edge technology RFID and the lasted contactless IC card protocol, according to the rules of transport operators and a series of complex statistical algorithms.

Our validators have many advantages, such as compact design, good scalability, low power consumption, real-time data communication, etc., which is an ideal choice for improving the operational management level and realizing informatization management. Consumers only need an authorized IC card, and the payment process can be quickly done with a tap; while the background system does the information processing, unified distribution of IC card, authorization, recharge and other work. Operators can easily query the entire system operation status through the background, and then perform some adjustments.

Emperor Tech has developed a variety of validators for different scenarios, including fixed and handheld versions. Perfect support for multiple payment methods such as bus passes, bank cards, QR codes, NFC devices and face recognition payments. It allows our validators to be integrated into a variety of different systems to serve more operational management.
