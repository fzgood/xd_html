Secure Identity Document

In the past 20 years, Emperor Tech has consistently regarded secure identity as the company's main development business. The company played an important role in numerous projects such as National Second-generation ID Card Project, National e-Passport Project, Kazakhstan e-ID Card & e-Passport Project, Hong Kong Digital Identity Project, and Hong Kong & Macao Home-visit Permit, that have all achieved great success.

The company's self-developed ID card & passport personalization system has outstanding performance in various projects, high stability and great efficiency improvement have been highly praised by customers.

The company has spent many years concentrating on the development of color printing technology on PC and enhanced anti-counterfeiting printing technology, which were widely applied on over 100 million PC identity documents worldwide with impressive customer’s satisfactions.
