Solution overview

Our physical world has been moving rapidly toward digitization, and the monotonous life is now full of diversity and fun.
Now, everyone has a digital identity. Thanks to artificial intelligence and the Internet of things, we can use facial recognition to confirm our identity without checking ID cards. As long as we got a mobile phone by hand, an APP or QR code would meet various payment scenarios in our life, such as taking bus, subway, shopping, etc. Based on our digital identity, we can even participate in elections on our mobile phone.
This is what Emperor Tech has been doing, based on trusted identity platforms, to provide people with interesting and convenient applications for life.
