Solutions>Smart Service>Application Kiosk

Self-service kiosk system has been a very mature solution, due to its convenience, high efficiency, real-time and small area covering, it is widely used in the government, medical care, finance, education and other industries. Focusing on the field of trusted identity, the Emperor Tech launched application kiosk which is mainly used for identity related enrollment, verification, document application, etc., Combined with the independently developed cloud management system, to jointly provide services for the government and banking departments.

Our system is highly secure. It has disaster recovery mechanism with system image backup. The servers are distributed, once the main server is down, the standby server starts immediately. The system encrypts important data in the DMZ, and multiple levels of firewall effectively protect the security of system. All these basically eliminate the possibility of system hacking. Since the self-service cabinet requires very high timeliness of information, our solution adopts load balancing mechanism, that all data can be transmitted with the fastest efficiency.

The whole software management system consists of two parts, the background centralized management software and front-end display software. Background management mainly includes four functions, system management, equipment management, software upgrade and data query; and display software on the client side is mainly responsible for the interaction with users, information collection and transmission.
