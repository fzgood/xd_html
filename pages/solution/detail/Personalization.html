Personalization

As the industrial automation technology has been furtherly improved, people are more willing to choose the whole automation solution with higher efficiency and better product quality rather than a single device, personalization system is one of the cases. With decades of experience on research and development of personalized system in the security certificate industry, Emperor Tech successfully developed a full range of color personalization systems to meet most personalized scenarios, which can be widely used in ID card, driver license, exit & entry pass, passport and other documents.

All systems use modular design, compared with traditional products, they are much easier to customize and upgrade according to customer needs. In general, the whole system consists of four main parts, Chip Encoding Module, Laser Engraving Module, UV Inkjet Module, QC Module. All the processes are controlled by the central computer intelligently, which can not only avoid the trouble of manual adjustment but also ensure the accuracy of production.
