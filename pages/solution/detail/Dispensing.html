Dispensing

Dispensing Solution is an important part in End-to-end Secure ID Document Management System, which is mainly responsible for sorting, dispensing. This innovative ID management solution was developed for maximize workflow optimization and user satisfaction with effective automation and world-class quality assurance across different biometric enrollment and identity verification processes.

The whole software management system consists of two parts, the background centralized management software and front-end display software. Background management mainly includes four functions, system management, equipment management, software upgrade and data query; and display software on the client side is mainly responsible for the interaction with users, information collection and transmission.

Emperor Tech's highly automated and reliable dispensing and instant issuance technologies can be integrated into different ID document management solutions, providing secure and trusted ID document dispensing, renewal, replacement and issuance services for government agencies around the world to better serve their citizens.
