Contactless smart pay
EMP5210 is a contactless smart card payment terminal developed for the public transportation fare payment system. It supports contactless smart card payment including RFID card, CPU card, banking IC card etc. With unrivaled security and encryption features, EMP5210 Ticket Validator provides the most versatile and efficient payment process.
With the custom applications developed for the government or bank, the kiosk can capture the applicant's identity information, verify the applicant's identity, collect supplementary documents and collect payment if necessary.
