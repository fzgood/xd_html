Instant card(sbi)
EMPTECH Service Kiosks enable citizens and customers to submit eID document applications or to open a bank account all at a self-service style. With the custom applications developed for the government or bank, the kiosk can capture the applicant's identity information, verify the applicant's identity, collect supplementary documents and collect payment if necessary.
EMPTECH Service Kiosks are designed to provide digitalized services for the government public service centers and banks with the aim of improving citizen & customer experience with management efficiency.
