Online / Offline Service

In recent years, rapid population growth has brought great management pressure to the government's public service agencies. We often see long queues for tickets, shopping, personal services or banking especially in densely populated cities. Slow and complicated handling of affairs has become the focus of people's attention and needs to be solved urgently.

The arrival of the Internet has brought hope to everyone, fast development of the Internet has accelerated the informatization construction of relevant departments. Thus, online & offline services come into being. Emperor Tech Online & Offline Service solution mainly focuses on the government business such as application, renewal, report for loss of various ID documents.

Applicants first need to submit personal information online, and they will get the application receipt after the management system verified the data. According to the current application situation of the whole city, the system will intelligently recommend the appropriate time and acceptance point to allocate resources reasonably, then send relevant information to the applicants’ mobile phone, so as to effectively avoid queuing and a large number of personnel congestions. At last, the applicants go to the acceptance point, complete the payment then can get their ID documents.

The online reservation system developed by Emperor Tech has user friendly interface. Applicants can freely choose the time and place according to their own conditions, and the capacity of different acceptance points will be clearly marked on the interface. Easy operation with clear instructions. After the reservation is done, the applicant can also check the relevant information through the mobile phone or computer in real time.
