Solutions>Smart Service>Bus Surveillance System

The rapid development of the city brings great convenience to everyone. As an important part of urban transportation, public transport becomes the first choice for citizens to travel. The safety problem of public transportation is what we can't ignore. In various of earlier video surveillance solutions, people paid more attention to the passengers in the bus. The increasing cars on the road and more complex traffic conditions require a more comprehensive bus surveillance system to ensure the safety of drivers and passengers.

The bus surveillance system of Emperor Tech can not only check the status of passengers in the car in real time, but also realize the monitoring of drivers. The driving state of the driver is the key to security. Through automatic regular detection, the operators can easily detect whether the driver is in fatigue driving or illegal driving. Once abnormal state is detected, the system will automatically alarm the driver with voice, and remote operating platforms can also warn the driver directly by radio.

Moreover, we also integrate advanced driving assistance system and 360 Camera. Large buses often have blind spots where the driver cannot see from the rearview mirror the mirror on both sides of the bus. 360-degree monitoring and a variety of sensors perfectly solve this problem, the driver can see the whole situation outside the car through the monitor. In addition, all videos will be uploaded to the background server regularly, and bus operators will check the performance of drivers to conduct assessment and training.
