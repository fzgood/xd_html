Solutions>Election>MLP

As we all know, color photography has been invented for over 150 years and it became popular in 1960s. Black-and-white Laser Photo Engraving Technology on Polycarbonate (PC) Data page has been deployed worldwide for over 20 years in the history of ID and passport personalization, and people are expecting advanced and innovative technologies to upgrade the current design for higher needs of security. Emperor Technology devoted 6 years to successfully develop cutting-edge color printing technology and enhanced anti-counterfeiting printing technology, which were widely applied on over 100 million PC ID and travel documents worldwide with impressive customer’s satisfactions.

The printing technology is upgraded by a smart combination of color printing and laser engraving. The effect of high-resolution color printing is far more than other color solutions, not only ensures high quality photos, bus also retains the advantages of laser engraving. What’s more, we use an optical variable features material over the color photo layer, which can be designed by letters or lines to improve the anti-counterfeit. Finally, we can provide a highly-customized epoxy overlay. It enhances the durability and security of various ID documents.
